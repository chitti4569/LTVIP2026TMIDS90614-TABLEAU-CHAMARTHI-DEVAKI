About the Project

iRevolution is a data analytics project that explores the growing influence of Apple’s iPhone in the Indian smartphone market.

  • Market share analysis
  • Sales trends across years
  • Consumer price preferences
  • Regional performance insights

The project uses Tableau for visualization and Flask for web integration.
